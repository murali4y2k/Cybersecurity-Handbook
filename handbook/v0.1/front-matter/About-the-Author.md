## My Journey

Welcome, and thank you for taking the time to read this handbook.

My name is **Murali Konduru**, and I am passionate about cybersecurity, secure software development, and helping others understand security through practical, real-world learning.

Like many professionals entering this field, I discovered that cybersecurity is vast, constantly evolving, and often overwhelming for beginners. Valuable information exists everywhere—official documentation, research papers, blogs, conference talks, online courses, and community discussions—but it is rarely organized into a single learning journey.

That realization inspired me to create this handbook.

---

## Why I Created This Handbook

This project began with a simple objective:

> Build the cybersecurity resource that I wished I had when I started learning.

Rather than collecting isolated notes, I wanted to create a structured guide that explains not only *what* security concepts are, but also *why* they matter, *how* they work in enterprise environments, and *where* they are applied in real-world scenarios.

My goal is to bridge the gap between theory, hands-on practice, and professional experience.

## Learning Philosophy

I believe cybersecurity is best learned through:

- Understanding first principles.
- Practicing in safe, authorized environments.
- Studying real-world enterprise architectures.
- Asking questions and staying curious.
- Continuously improving through hands-on experience.

Knowledge becomes meaningful only when it can be applied.
