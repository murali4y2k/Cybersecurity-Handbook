# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We, as contributors and maintainers of the Cybersecurity Handbook, pledge to foster an open, welcoming, respectful, and inclusive community.

We are committed to providing a positive experience for everyone, regardless of:

- Age
- Body size
- Disability
- Ethnicity
- Gender identity and expression
- Level of experience
- Nationality
- Personal appearance
- Race
- Religion
- Sexual identity and orientation

Our goal is to build a professional learning community where knowledge is shared respectfully.

---

# Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment include:

- Being respectful and professional.
- Accepting constructive feedback.
- Helping new contributors.
- Sharing knowledge generously.
- Focusing on technical discussions.
- Encouraging collaboration.

---

# Unacceptable Behavior

The following behaviors are unacceptable:

- Harassment
- Personal attacks
- Hate speech
- Trolling
- Intimidation
- Publishing private information
- Deliberate misinformation
- Disruptive behavior

---

# Cybersecurity Ethics

This project promotes:

- Ethical hacking
- Responsible disclosure
- Authorized security testing
- Secure software development
- Defensive security

This project does **not** support:

- Illegal hacking
- Malware distribution
- Credential theft
- Unauthorized access
- Piracy
- Cybercrime

Educational content must always be presented responsibly and within legal and ethical boundaries.

---

# Community Responsibilities

Maintainers are responsible for:

- Reviewing contributions fairly.
- Enforcing this Code of Conduct.
- Maintaining a welcoming environment.
- Encouraging constructive collaboration.

Contributors are responsible for:

- Following project guidelines.
- Respecting community members.
- Maintaining professionalism.
- Reporting inappropriate behavior.

---

# Reporting Issues

If you experience or witness behavior that violates this Code of Conduct, please report it privately to the project maintainers.

Reports will be handled respectfully and confidentially whenever possible.

---

# Enforcement

Project maintainers may take appropriate action, including:

- Warning contributors
- Editing or removing inappropriate content
- Temporarily restricting participation
- Permanently banning repeat offenders

The response will be proportionate to the severity of the behavior.
